Talavera Tempranillo - Merlot, Tinto Cariñena
Aragon region. Tempranillo/merlot grapes. Notes of fruits of the forest mixed with sweet spice.

Panuelo Tempranillo - Carbernet Sauvignon
Tempranillo/cabernet sauvignon grapes. Ripe and dark fruit flavours.

Casa Albali Tempranillo - Shiraz, Valdepeñas
Castilla La Mancha region. Tempranillo/syrah/shiraz grapes. Plenty of ripe cherry and plum fruit flavours.

Rioja region. 1005 tempranillo. Aged for 12 months. Notes of ripened black fruits and aromas including cocoa and nutmeg.
